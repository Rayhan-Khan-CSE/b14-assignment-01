Who should attend DevConf 2026?

Developers, software engineers, students, tech leads, startup founders, and anyone passionate about technology.

Will session recordings be available?

Yes. All registered attendees will receive access to session recordings after the conference.

Are meals included?

Lunch, refreshments, and networking breaks are included with all conference passes.

Yes. Ticket transfers are allowed up to 7 days before the event begins.
